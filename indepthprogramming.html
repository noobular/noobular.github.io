Game Maker Skills Explanation

Sprite Animation
To create an animation with a sprite, you create many frames or sprites all located on a single sprite with each of them slightly moving to do the desired animation, and when they are played back like a flip book they are animated and play out the action
Movement
Movement, is very specifically engine based, with game maker they way to move an object is to either increase its x/y value or you increase its hspeed, negative numbers with this variable will go left, and positive will go right.
